Integrated Genetics

This Minecraft modification adds more interoperability between the Integrated Dynamics and Forestry for Minecraft (Forestry) mods.

Specifically it adds new Forestry bees which make Integrated Dynamics materials and Integrated Dynamics parts which can read the genetic information on Forestry Bees.
